Leadership Open Source Announcement

OpenRockets Foundation Leadership Transition

Note: This is an exact copy of the official announcement published on the OpenRockets Foundation website and LinkedIn page, written by the Outreach Manager of OpenRockets Foundation.

OFFICIAL ANNOUNCEMENT

The OpenRockets Open-source Software Foundation announces a leadership transition. Neksha DeSilva has officially resigned from the position of Executive Director and Founder, effective immediately. Mr./Ms. DeSilva is no longer affiliated with the organization in any capacity.

About Neksha DeSilva's Contributions:

Neksha DeSilva served as the Executive Director and Founder of OpenRockets Open-source Software Foundation. With a passion for open-source software, Neksha was instrumental in shaping the foundation's vision and direction. Under their leadership, the foundation grew to become a hub for innovation and collaboration in the open-source community.

Neksha's expertise in software development and commitment to open-source principles were key drivers in the foundation's success. They played a pivotal role in fostering a culture of transparency, inclusivity, and community engagement within the organization. As founder and contributor, Neksha guided the foundation's projects and initiatives, ensuring that they aligned with the core values of open-source software.

Their leadership advanced the foundation's mission and inspired countless individuals to contribute to the open-source ecosystem. The foundation expresses gratitude for Neksha's dedication and contributions during their tenure.

Recruitment for New Executive Director:

OpenRockets Foundation is now seeking a qualified individual to assume the role of Executive Director. The ideal candidate must meet the following requirements:

  • Minimum age: 18 years or older
  • Educational qualification: Bachelor of Science degree or equivalent
  • Strong commitment to open-source principles and community leadership

Interested candidates are invited to submit their CV/resume to: official@openrockets.com

The foundation looks forward to welcoming new leadership to continue its mission of advancing open-source software innovation and collaboration.

— Outreach Manager, OpenRockets Foundation
